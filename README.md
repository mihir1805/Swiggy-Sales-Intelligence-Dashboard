# 🍔 Swiggy Sales Intelligence Dashboard

An interactive Excel-based analytics dashboard designed to analyze Swiggy sales performance, customer behavior, food preferences, and business trends through KPI-driven visualizations and insights.

---

## 📌 Project Overview

This project focuses on transforming raw Swiggy sales data into a dynamic and interactive dashboard using Microsoft Excel. The dashboard provides meaningful insights into sales performance, order behavior, ratings, food preferences, and regional trends to support data-driven decision-making.

The project simulates a real-world business analytics workflow where raw data is cleaned, transformed, analyzed, and visualized to generate actionable business insights.

---

## 🎯 Objectives

- Analyze total sales and order performance
- Track customer ratings and order trends
- Identify top-performing cities and states
- Understand food category preferences
- Monitor weekly, monthly, and quarterly trends
- Build an interactive dashboard for business decision-making

---

## 🛠 Tools & Technologies Used

- Microsoft Excel

- Data Cleaning & Transformation

## 🔍 Key Business Insights

### Customer Preferences
Non-Veg food contributed significantly more revenue compared to Veg orders, indicating stronger customer demand in that segment.

### Geographic Insights
Bengaluru emerged as the highest revenue-generating city, showing strong market demand and customer activity.

### Customer Satisfaction
The average customer rating of 4.34 suggests overall positive customer experience and service quality.

### Sales Trends
Sales remained relatively stable across months with some seasonal fluctuations and demand spikes.

### Order Behavior
Weekly trends indicate recurring customer activity patterns that can help optimize marketing campaigns.
